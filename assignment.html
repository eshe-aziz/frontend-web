My First Week at AkiraChix

Introduction

During my first week at AkiraChix, I was welcomed into a vibrant community dedicated to empowering young women in technology. I've had the opportunity to embark myself on a vibrant learning environment and connect with passionate individuals in the tech community from different communities in Kenya and different countries in East Africa. The week started off with an orientation session where we got to understand the core values and what is expected from us.

Good things that I experienced

  1. Introduction to the units.
  2. Creation of a new gmail account.
  3. Meeting our trainers and hearing their expectations.
  4. Learning new programming laanguages I never came across with.

Not-so-good things I experienced

  • Coping up with the new environment and routine.
  • Difficulty in understanding new techonology concepts.
  • Struggling to keep up with the pace of the training program.
  • Having less time to check up my family.
